About Us

At Wheeler HVAC, we believe comfort shouldn’t be complicated. Since 2020, we’ve been helping homeowners and small businesses across North Alabama stay cool in the summer, warm in the winter, and confident their systems will keep running when they need them most.

Our team of certified and insured technicians brings decades of combined experience to every job, whether it’s installing a new high-efficiency system, troubleshooting a late-night breakdown, or performing routine maintenance to extend the life of your equipment.

We’re a locally owned company built on trust, transparency, and top-quality service. When you call Wheeler HVAC, you’re not just a customer — you’re a neighbor, and we’ll treat your home like it’s our own.
